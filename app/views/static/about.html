About

Edinburgh Collected is managed and maintained by Edinburgh Libraries, part of the City of Edinburgh Council (CEC).

Edinburgh Collected is for anyone who wants to collect, share and explore memories related to the story of Edinburgh, creating history for now and the future.

All the material added will become part of Edinburgh Libraries digital heritage collections of the City, adding to the material held in the Central Library, already the most extensive collection about Edinburgh in existence.

You can

  • collect and share material related to life in Edinburgh, both past and present
  • Upload memories that are images, (from early 2015 written, audio and document formats will also be available)
  • Manage and personalise your content, and collect it in scrapbooks

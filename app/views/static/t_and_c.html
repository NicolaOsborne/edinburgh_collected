About Edinburgh Scrapbooks

Edinburgh scrapbooks is managed and maintained by Edinburgh Libraries, part of the City of Edinburgh Council (CEC) for everyone who wants to collect, preserve and access material related to family and community heritage to share with current and future generations.

  • You can collect and share material online related to life in the city of Edinburgh from the past, present and for the future
  • Upload a range of content including images ( audio and documents from early 2015)
  • Manage and personalise your content, gather it into scrapbooks to create and edit your own stories

Terms of Use

By using this site you are agreeing to the terms of use.

All users can view published content on the website.

If you want to add content you must register to create an acount, providing accurate, current and complete information aboutyourself and undertake to keep this information up to date. The information you will be required to supply is your name, email and your user name. Only your user name will appear on the website.

It is your responsibility to keep your user account information safe and confidential.

We will only request information necessary for you to register on this system and will only use it to contact you about Edinburgh Scrapbooks or other library online services.

Be aware that uploading all types of content eg images will use the data allowance for your device when using 3G or 4G.

Groups

Groups eg local history societies and community groups can have a group account. A member of the group will be an administrator who will give permission for other users to add material to a group area. The group adminstrator will be responsible for giving and withdrawing access.

Moderation

All new content added to the site is moderated, if content is unsuitable it will not be published to the site and the user notified. In some instances low risk content may be published whilst awaiting moderation. (Moderation is under development this section will be updated as new features are implemented)

The City of Edinburgh Council claims no ownership or rights over material added, this remains with the account holder who can delete content at any time. By submitting content account holders are agreeing that it can be used for personal, educational and non commercial purposes. The following examples of potential uses is by way of illustration, it is not a complete list.

  • Printing of content
  • Downloading of content
  • Re-use by the City of Edinburgh Council to promote its heritage collections
  • Addition of content to www.capitalcollections.org.uk or www.ourtownstories.co.uk or any future online channel developed by Edinburgh Libraries
  • Reuse by other online channels eg local history website, social media channels
  • Reuse by others to create new non-commercial digital products

If you believe that any material on Edinburgh Scrapbooks infringes the IP of any other person contact informationdigital@edinburgh.gov.uk

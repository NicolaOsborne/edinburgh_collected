Privacy and Cookies DRAFT

Our aims

The Council takes your privacy seriously. We always aim to follow the guidance provided by the Information Commissioner, and to comply with the Data Protection Act.

When you submit content to this site, any personal data only appears in relation to your content and is not re-used for any purpose by the Council.

Cookies

The website does store cookies on your computer, but they do not contain personal or other data that could be used to identify you.

In line with our Information Governance policies, we aim to archive or delete data from our systems when it's no longer actively useful for providing the relevant service to you.

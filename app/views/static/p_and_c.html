Privacy and Cookies DRAFT

Our aims

The Council takes your privacy seriously. We always aim to follow the guidance provided by the Information Commissioner, and to comply with our general privacy policy.

When you submit a comment to this site any personal data only appears in relation to your content and is not re-used for any purpose by the Council.

Cookies

The web site does store cookies on your computer, but these do not contain personal data or anything that could identify you to others.

In line with our Records policy, we aim to archive or delete data from our systems when it's no longer actively useful for providing the relevant service to you.
